Kenya Stopover is a premium hospitality and tourism company dedicated to transforming transit experiences through Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA), Nairobi, into memorable encounters with Kenyan warmth and culture. We understand that travel can be exhausting, and layovers often feel like lost time. Kenya Stopover redefines the transit experience, positioning Kenya as the ultimate home of welcoming hospitality, even for the briefest of visits.
Our Mission To enrich the transit experience for travelers passing through JKIA by providing seamless assistance, genuine Kenyan hospitality, and curated short-term excursion opportunities, thereby showcasing Kenya as a vibrant and welcoming destination.
Our Vision To be the leading provider of transit hospitality services at JKIA, recognized globally for our exceptional service, efficiency, and ability to leave a lasting positive impression of Kenya on every traveler.
